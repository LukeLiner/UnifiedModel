# Code Of Conduct

UModel is an open-source technical community. We want discussions, issues, pull requests, and reviews to be useful, respectful, and grounded in evidence.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but clarify when behavior causes harm or confusion.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on facts, tradeoffs, and project goals.
- Give maintainers and contributors enough context to reproduce issues.
- Credit ideas, reports, and contributions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Spam, commercial abuse, or coordinated bad-faith activity.
- Publicly disclosing security vulnerabilities before maintainers can triage them.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate, edit, hide, or remove content that violates this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent loss of participation privileges.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers through the hosting organization's maintainer contact path. Security issues should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), not this conduct process.
